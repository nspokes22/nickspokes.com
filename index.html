ABOUT ME

Welcome to my website! I am a student and aspiring software engineer from Purcellville, Virginia, who's passionate about all things technology. My first foray into the computer world was in middle school when I built my first computer with the help of my friend, and I loved the process so much that I decided I would pursue a computer related career. Over the years, I've followed my passion experimenting with different technologies and have made numerous personal projects, and ultimately, I found that I enjoyed software development the most. After graduating from high school, I attended James Madison University where I continue to study computer science with the hopes of soon starting my career as a software developer.
